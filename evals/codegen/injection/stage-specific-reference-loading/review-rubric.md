# Review Rubric

## Passing Standard

A passing solution produces explicit, bounded evidence for Stage Specific Reference Loading and satisfies the case assertions without relying on inferred compliance. The final handoff must name validation evidence, changed files or evidence files, and residual risk.

## Scoring

- 30 percent capability-specific evidence completeness.
- 25 percent professional routing, planning, or review judgment appropriate to the case.
- 20 percent validation evidence and stale-evidence avoidance.
- 15 percent bounded telemetry, privacy, and security discipline.
- 10 percent maintainability and minimal-correct implementation judgment.

## Automatic Failure Conditions

- Missing required assertion-backed capability evidence.
- Treating inferred or fallback process text as explicit compliance.
- Claiming completion without validation evidence or residual risk.
- Leaking raw prompts, secrets, full command output, or local absolute user paths.
- Using cost reduction or efficiency language as a quality result in this phase.

## Reviewer Notes

Review the final artifacts first, then verify that process and route evidence is explicit enough for machines to inspect. Do not award credit for broad professional-sounding prose unless it maps to the specific assertion terms for this case.
