# Threat Modeling Checklist

- Identify protected assets and security objectives.
- Identify actors, including attackers, users, admins, services, and third parties.
- Map trust boundaries, entry points, and data flows.
- Enumerate abuse cases and threat scenarios.
- Rate impact and likelihood for high-risk paths.
- Define mitigations with implementation owner and verification evidence.
- Define monitoring, audit, alerting, and incident visibility for critical threats.
- Record residual risk owner, rationale, and review or escalation decision.
