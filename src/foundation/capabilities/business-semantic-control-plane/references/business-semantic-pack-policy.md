# Business Semantic Pack Policy

Business Semantic Packs are precision artifacts for one task. They include:

- `task_business_intent`
- `business_vocabulary`
- `business_objects`
- `business_rules`
- `workflows`
- `data_and_signal_semantics`
- `code_mapping`
- `memory_projection`
- `validation_map`
- `context_control`

Every section may be brief, but it must either contain evidence-backed claims or explicitly record why the section is not applicable. Do not fill sections with generic business explanations.

Evidence class policy:

- `FACT`: current source, user-provided source, owner review, or validation result.
- `INFERENCE`: reasoned from facts but not directly stated.
- `ASSUMPTION`: accepted temporarily with owner/risk.
- `OPEN_QUESTION`: unresolved and blocking or non-blocking.
- `MEMORY_SIGNAL`: project memory signal that requires current-source confirmation.

Graph and memory may select files, rules, workflows, or tests to inspect. They do not prove business meaning alone.
