# Business Golden Case Policy

A business golden case is a durable, business-facing validation record. It must map a business claim to:

- Given inputs and context.
- When action or transition.
- Then expected business outcome.
- Negative cases or forbidden transitions when material.
- Validation command, test name, owner review, or residual risk.

Golden cases do not replace unit, integration, contract, or E2E tests. They identify which business claims those tests or reviews must prove.
