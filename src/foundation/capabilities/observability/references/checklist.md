# Observability Checklist

- Identify user impact, operational owner, and investigation path.
- Add structured privacy-safe logs with correlation or trace context.
- Define metrics for volume, errors, latency, saturation, and outcomes.
- Add trace spans at meaningful service, job, database, and external boundaries.
- Define dashboards and actionable alerts with thresholds and owners.
- Verify signals in test, staging, or post-release checks.
