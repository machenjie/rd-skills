# Architecture Tradeoff Analysis Checklist

- State the decision context and decision status.
- List hard constraints and ranked decision forces.
- Include viable options and at least one rejected alternative.
- Explain why the selected option wins under current constraints.
- Record consequences for delivery, testing, operations, security, and future change.
- Identify risks, mitigations, residual risk, and owner.
- Define verification evidence and rollout assumptions.
- Define reassessment trigger or expiry condition.
- Keep the output ADR-ready and concise.
